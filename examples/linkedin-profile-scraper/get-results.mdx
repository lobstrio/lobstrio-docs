---
title: "Get Results"
description: "Retrieve scraped data from Linkedin Profile Scraper"
api: "GET https://api.lobstr.io/v1/results"
---

Retrieve scraped data from your **Linkedin Profile Scraper** runs.

## Headers
<ParamField header="Authorization" type="string" required>
  Your API authentication token. Value: `Token YOUR_API_KEY`
</ParamField>

## Query Parameters
<ParamField query="squid" type="string" required>
  Hash of the squid to get results from
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="run" type="string">
  Hash of a specific run (optional)
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="page" type="integer">
  Page number (default: 1). Example: `1`
</ParamField>

## Result Fields
Each result object contains the following fields:

<ResponseField name="url" type="string">
  Direct URL to the LinkedIn profile. Example: `https://www.linkedin.com/in/david-shibley`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="public_identifier" type="string">
  LinkedIn public profile slug used in the profile URL. Example: `david-shibley`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="first_name" type="string">
  First name of the LinkedIn member. Example: `David`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="last_name" type="string">
  Last name of the LinkedIn member. Example: `Shibley`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="full_name" type="string">
  Full name of the LinkedIn member. Example: `David Shibley`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="email" type="string">
  Email address found or enriched for this LinkedIn member. Example: `dave@melcap.com`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="email_status" type="string">
  Validation status of the email address (e.g. valid, risky, invalid). Example: `valid`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="headline" type="string">
  Professional headline shown below the name on the LinkedIn profile. Example: `Vice President of Business Strategy at Cassidy Advertising`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="description" type="string">
  About or summary text from the LinkedIn profile. Example: `With over 5 years of professional client services experience, currently serving as Vice President of Business Strategy at Cassidy Advertising & Consulting, contributing to strategic planning and client engagement. Committed to fostering collaborative growth and aligning strategies to advance organizational goals. Past roles as Investment Banking Associate and Staff Accountant honed expertise in financial data analysis, reporting, and deal structuring. Skilled in business strategy, client relations, and leveraging insights and analytics for impactful outcomes.`
